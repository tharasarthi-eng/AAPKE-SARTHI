My Vision for Aapke Sarthi

Mathematics is often seen as a collection of formulas to be memorized. My philosophy is different: Mathematics is about understanding the "Why" behind every step.

As an alumnus of IIT Kharagpur, I have seen first-hand the level of conceptual clarity required to crack exams like JEE. Aapke Sarthi is my initiative to bring that same clarity to every student, organized topic-by-topic so you never feel overwhelmed.

"My goal is not just to teach you how to solve a problem, but to train your mind to think like a mathematician."
